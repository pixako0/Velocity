The controls of the game are:
- W/Up: Accelerate your ship into the direction the ship is facing
- A/Left: Turn your ship left
- D/Right: Turn your ship right
- Space: Shoot a rocket
- Enter: Respawn during death screen

The goal of the game is to gain as high of a `score` as possible by shooting other ships, but the more ships you destroy the more ships appear to hunt you, my highest score is 38 have fun trying to beat that :D.
